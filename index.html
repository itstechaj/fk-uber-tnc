  1. Eligibility

Participation in this Offer is free but limited to individuals who:

(a) Have a valid Flipkart account;
(b) Have a valid Uber account in India;
(c) Successfully link their Flipkart account and Uber account (a "Linked Account");
(d) Ensure both accounts remain active and in good standing at the time of earning and crediting of Supercoins; and

(e) are of at least 18 years of age;

(f) Reside in India

Linking must be completed prior to booking the Eligible Ride. Rides completed before successful account linking shall not qualify for this Offer.

Only one Uber account may be linked to one Flipkart account.

Uber reserves the right to determine eligibility at its sole discretion.

  1. Definitions

For purposes of this Offer:

"Linked Account" means a Flipkart account that has been successfully linked with an Uber rider account within the Uber App or Flipkart App.

"Eligible Ride" means a completed Uber ride booked through the Uber App in India, paid for by the Linked Account holder, excluding cancelled trips.

"Supercoins" means reward units issued under Flipkart's Supercoins Program, governed exclusively by Flipkart's Supercoins terms available at [•].

"Trip Fare" means the total amount reflected on the Uber App at the time of booking of an eligible ride, where such amount shall exclude any promotional codes, discounts, taxes, tips, fees, tolls, booking fee, convenience fee, parking fees or airport fees.

Users with a Linked Account, you would be entitled to earn Supercoins with each Uber ride, subject to the terms set out below.

  • Base Earn (All Eligible Users): Users with a Linked Account will earn Supercoins equivalent to 4% of the Trip Fare, capped at 150 supercoins per Eligible Ride.

  • New Uber User Bonus: Users who have never taken a trip on Uber OR have not taken an Uber trip in the past 365 days will receive extra 150 Supercoins when they link their Flipkart account and complete an Uber Eligible Ride from their Linked Account within 28 days of linking.

  • Resurrected Uber Bonus: Users who have not taken a ride with Uber in the last 84 to 365 days will receive extra 100 SuperCoins on Account linking, provided they take an Uber Eligible Ride from their Linked Account within 28 days of linking.

  • Limited Time Bonus: Users who link their Uber and Flipkart accounts between 15 Apr 2026 to 29 Apr 2026 [2 weeks], will receive 50 bonus Supercoins, provided at least one Eligible Ride is completed within 28 days of linking.
